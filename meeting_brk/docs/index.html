Plan and Record Meeting

Prepare agenda, send mail and record minutes

Install

From your site

To install this app, login to your site and click on "Installer". Search for Meeting Brk and click on "Install"

Using Bench

Go to your bench folder and setup the new app

$ bench get-app meeting_brk https://github.com/frappe/meeting_brk
$ bench new-site testsite
$ bench --site testsite install-app meeting_brk

Login to your site to configure the app.
